# GOTTLOB FREGE · IX

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

GOTTLOB FREGE (1848–1925) — LOGIKĒ IX, the founder of MODERN logic, the biggest advance since Aristotle. For 2,000 years logic could say 'all men are mortal' but NOT 'every number has a larger one' — the missing piece was QUANTITY. In the Begriffsschrift (1879) Frege supplied it: ⚑ QUANTIFIERS (∀ 'for all', ∃ 'there exists'), BOUND VARIABLES, and the analysis of a statement as a FUNCTION of arguments (Mortal(Socrates), not subject-predicate) — so logic could finally express the sentences of mathematics, and later the queries of a database & the types of a program. This is FIRST-ORDER (predicate) logic. LIVE: (1) ∀x/∃x over a clickable domain of red/blue objects (the thing term logic couldn't do — quantifiers + connectives, incl. the tautology ∀x(Red∨Blue) & contradiction ∃x(Red∧Blue)); (2) RELATIONS — a clickable R(x,y) matrix where ∀x∃y ('everyone relates to someone', true) ≠ ∃y∀x ('someone everyone relates to', false) — same symbols, swapped order, different claim (verified); (3) the RUSSELL PARADOX — 'the set of all sets that don't contain themselves: is R∈R?' → contradiction either way (clickable), the letter that wrecked his life's work in 1902. CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG (predicate logic/quantifiers — foundational & permanent, THE logic of modern math & CS) / COLLAPSED (his LOGICISM — math = pure logic — destroyed by Russell's paradox as Grundgesetze vol.2 was at the printer; refounded later on type theory/ZFC) / IGNORED-THEN-OWNED (almost unread in life — flows down only via Russell/Wittgenstein/Carnap; ⚠ HONEST: his 1924 diaries record virulent antisemitic & anti-democratic views — the logic stands, the man does not).
